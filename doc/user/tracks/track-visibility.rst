.. _track-visibility:

Track Visibility
================

The track visibility tab in the :ref:`left-panel` allows
toggling the visibility of tracks.

.. image:: /_static/img/track-visibility.png
   :align: center

Tracks can also be quickly hidden with :menuselection:`Right click --> Hide`.
